Gluten Free Vanilla Cake

    2 1/4 cups all purpose gluten-free flour with xanthan gum
  • 1/4 cup cornstartch
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegtable oil
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

instructions

Preheat oven to 350F. Adjust racks to be in the middle position for best results. Line two 8-inch round cake pans wu=ith partchment paper andf set aside

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour blend, cornstartch, baking powder, baking soda and salt.

In another bowl mix the eggs granulated sugar, vegtable oil, melted butter, buttermilk, baking soda, and salt

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until the batter is smooth and well combined. Avoid over mixing to prevent the batter from becoming tough

Pour the batter into the prepared cake pans (about 671g per pan) and bake in the pre heated oven for 25-30 minutes or untill a toothpick incerted into the center of the cake comes out clean.

Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes (so it keeps it's shape). Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool compleatly

Once the cake is fully cooled, you can decorate it as you please.
