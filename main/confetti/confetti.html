Butterscotch confetti

  • Butter or margarine - 1/4 cup
  • Peanut butter 1/2 cup
  • Butterscotch chips - 1 cup
  • Small multicoloured marshmallows - 8 oz

Melt butter and peanut butter in a large sauce pan over low heat. Stir in chips until melted. Cool until you can hold your hand on the bottom of the pot. To speed up the process, stir while holding pan in cold water. Add marshmallows and stir until all are coated. Pack down into 9 x 9 - inch (22 x 22 cm) pan that has been lined with wax paper. Refrigerate. 36 squares.
